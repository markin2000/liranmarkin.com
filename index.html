In every family meeting during my early childhood my uncle used to ask complex riddles where everyone was struggling even after 10 minutes of thinking. It was known that when you’ll ask me, you’ll get the answer in less than a minute.

From learning colors and shapes at the age of 10 months, I grew up into starting my master’s degree while I’m 18. This is not just hard work… Today I’m surrounded by people who, just like me, obtained an early degree and on the top of their field. But when we talk about life it seems like I always see one level deeper, I take concepts from one field of study and apply them into life in general.

For me, math is abstract & analytical thinking and it is necessary for every aspect in life: career, finance, relationships, personal development and health.

Investing a decade into sharpening my learning skills was the smartest choice I could make. Today I feel safe to say that I can do anything, what I know is solved already and what I don’t know can be learned :)

I’m always happy to chat. Get in touch and let's solve something together :)
